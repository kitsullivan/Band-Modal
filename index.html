Hadden Sayers

Native-Texan Hadden Sayers is just as comfortable on a massive festival stage with a Stratocaster in his hands as he is strumming an acoustic guitar in your living room...
Equal parts blue-eyed soul and red-hot guitar. This is a mesmerizing multi-dimensional live performance. Hadden is a master of his instruments... but his passionate songwriting, which twists roots rock, blues and soul music into something completely fresh, is what sets him apart from the pack.
